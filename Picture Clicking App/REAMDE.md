# PicClick
It is a picture clicking app, where you can click and save image to your phone.

## Technology Used
- Android
- Java

## Requirements
- Android Version 5.0 and above
- CompileSdkVersion 30
- MinSdkVersion 21
- targetSdkVersion 30
- Androidx
