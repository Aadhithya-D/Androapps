# CustomListView
It is a CustomListView app that has its own adapter along with its own view design and onClickListner.

## Technology Used
- Android
- Java

## Requirements
- Android Version 5.0 and above
- CompileSdkVersion 30
- MinSdkVersion 21
- targetSdkVersion 30
- Androidx
